Jimmy Torres

Motivated veteran with experience in tactical communications and small team leadership. Undergraduate Cybersecurity Student at Metropolitan State University of Denver.

I was exposed to many sophisticated systems and methods for transmission and this interest has encouraged me to continue growing my knowledge for how the world communicates. My future goals consist of a career in technology while continuing to further my knowledge in networking, telecommunications, and cybersecurity. With continuing concern over our data and equipment gets used, I wish to be an asset for preventing any future attempts to compromise a system.
